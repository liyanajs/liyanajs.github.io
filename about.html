About Me.

I’m Liyana, a developer who loves the chaos of creation.

My path to engineering wasn't a straight line—it was a map I drew myself. It started in the world of GIS, using Python to convert files and build web maps. That sparked a curiosity for the web that led me to front-end development at a fast-paced startup, where I truly fell in love with code.


Today, I’m a Server Engineer in the gaming industry. It’s a space that lets me experiment freely across the full stack. I love the process of figuring out what works (and what definitely doesn’t) and carrying those lessons forward.


Outside of the terminal? I’m usually painting, nurturing my plants, or planning my next trip to explore the world—coffee in hand, of course.
